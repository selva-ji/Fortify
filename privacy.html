Privacy Policy for Fortify

Last Updated: 06.01.2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Fortify ("Application") for mobile devices.

Data Collection and Usage

Fortify is designed with your privacy in mind. Our goal is to collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve our service.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, contacts, or precise location.

Third-Party Services: Firebase Analytics

To understand how our application is used and to improve its performance and user experience, we use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google. Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage and device data, which may include:

  • Device information (such as model, operating system version).
  • Anonymized IP addresses.
  • In-app events (such as feature usage).
  • Session duration.

This data is aggregated and anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally. For more information on how Google collects and processes data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms page.

Permissions

Fortify requires the following permissions to function:

  • Device Administrator (BIND_DEVICE_ADMIN): This permission is required solely to enable the app to lock your device's screen. This is a core feature of the application.
  • Internet: This permission is required to allow Firebase Analytics to send anonymous usage data.
