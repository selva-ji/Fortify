Your privacy is our top priority. Fortify is designed to be a simple, secure, and transparent application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data from your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

Fortify does not collect, read, or store any of the following:

Permissions

Fortify requires the Device Administrator permission for its core functionality. Here is why this permission is necessary:

  • Device Administrator (BIND_DEVICE_ADMIN): This permission is required solely to enable the app to lock your device's screen. The app cannot perform any other administrative actions. This permission is only activated when you explicitly grant it within the app, and it can be revoked at any time through your device's system settings.

All operations are performed locally on your device. No information is sent to us or any third party.
