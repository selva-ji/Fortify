Fortify: Your Personal Lockdown Tool

Fortify gives you the power to instantly secure your device by disabling biometric authentication (fingerprint, face unlock) and forcing the use of your PIN, pattern, or password.

Why Use Fortify?

In a world where our phones hold our most sensitive information, biometric locks offer convenience but can also be a security risk. Fortify addresses these vulnerabilities head-on.

  • Protection Against Coercion: In a dangerous situation, a single tap ensures no one can force you to unlock your device with your face or fingerprint.
  • Secure Your Device While Sleeping: Prevent unauthorized access from others using your biometrics while you are asleep or incapacitated.
  • Ultimate Peace of Mind: Feel confident knowing you have a tool to immediately harden your device's security beyond its default settings.

Key Features

  • One-Tap Lockdown: Instantly secure your device without fumbling through settings.
  • Widget Control: Place the sleek widget on your home screen for quick access.
  • Quick Settings Tile: Add the tile to your quick settings panel for activation from any screen.
  • Simple and Focused: A clean, minimalist app that does one thing perfectly.
  • 100% Free and Ad-Free: No ads, no premium features, no distractions. Your security is our only priority.

How to Use Fortify

  1. Install Fortify from the Google Play Store.
  2. Open the app and follow the simple prompt to grant Device Administrator permission. This is necessary for the app to be able to lock your screen.
  3. Add the Fortify widget to your home screen and/or add the Quick Settings tile to your status bar panel.
  4. Simply tap the widget or tile to instantly lock your device and disable biometrics.
